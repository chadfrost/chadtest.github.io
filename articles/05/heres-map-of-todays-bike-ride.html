Heres Map Of Todays Bike Ride

June 25, 2005

Here’s a map of today’s bike ride… a mere 25 miles and 3800 ft of climbing.
But, I was riding my single-speed bike, with no suspension, not even the fork, so it counts for more! Ah, the joys of single-speed… only those with a masochistic streak need apply. The girl on a single-speed who left me in the dust (OK, it was mud) on last week’s ride motivated me to ride the thing more.
